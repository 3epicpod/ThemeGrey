Responsive Column

With the four tiers of grids available you're bound to run into issues where, at certain breakpoints, your columns don't clear quite right as one is taller than the other. To fix that, use a combination of a .clearfix and some responsive utility classes.
.col-xs-6 .col-sm-3
.col-xs-6 .col-sm-3 Resize your viewport or check it out on your phone for an example.
.col-xs-6 .col-sm-3
.col-xs-6 .col-sm-3 Resize your viewport or check it out on your phone for an example.
The below grids will alter their widths based upon the screen resolution. Resize the window to observe the responsive of below grids.
.col-lg-6 .col-md-12 .col-sm-6 .col-xs-12
.col-lg-6 .col-md-12 .col-sm-6 .col-xs-12
.col-lg-3 .col-md-1 .col-sm-5 .col-xs-3
.col-lg-4 .col-md-7 .col-sm-1 .col-xs-3
.col-lg-2 .col-md-2 .col-sm-4 .col-xs-3
.col-lg-3 .col-md-2 .col-sm-2 .col-xs-3

Offsetting columns

Move columns to the right using .col-md-offset-* classes. These classes increase the left margin of a column by * columns. For example, .col-md-offset-4 moves .col-md-4 over four columns.

1. The below grids will maintain their offset till it reaches the screen width of .col-sm-*
.col-md-4
.col-md-4 .col-md-offset-4
2. The below grids will maintain their offset till it reaches the screen width of .col-xs-*
.col-sm-3 .col-sm-offset-3
.col-sm-3 .col-sm-offset-3
3. The below grids will maintain their offset forever on all screen resolution.
.col-xs-6 .col-xs-offset-3
4. The below grids will maintain their offset according to thier screen resolution.

    If screen width is lg, it maintain col-lg-offset-*
    If screen width is md, it maintain col-md-offset-*
    If screen width is sm, it maintain col-sm-offset-*
    If screen width is xs, it maintain col-xs-offset-*
.col-xs-5 .col-lg-offset-6 .col-md-offset-4 .col-sm-offset-7 .col-xs-offset-2
